Peanut Butter Cake

Return Home

Cake Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup Softened Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 pack Butter Cake Mix
  • 2/3 cup Water

Cake Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325degrees.
  2. Grease bottom of two 9in round cake pans.
  3. Cream peanut butter and butter until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well with each.
  5. Alternately add sifted cake mix and water.
  6. Stir until just combined.
  7. Pour into pans and bake for 25 min.
  8. Allow to cool for 10 min before turning out onto cooling rack.
  9. Frost with peanut butter frosting.

Frosting Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Softened Butter
  • 1 cup Peanut Butter
  • 3 tbsp Milk (as needed)
  • 2 cups Powdered Sugar (sifted)

Frosting Instructions

  1. Beat butter and peanut butter well.
  2. Add sugar.
  3. Add 1 tbsp milk at a time as frosting begins to thicken.
  4. Beat for at least 3 min or until fluffy.
